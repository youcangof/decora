About This Collection

Welcome to Printable Wall Art Set – Volume 1, a curated selection of minimalist and modern quote designs created to bring warmth, calm, and inspiration to your space.

Each piece in this collection is thoughtfully designed to fit effortlessly into any room — from cozy corners and home offices to living rooms and entryways. Whether you're looking to add a subtle touch of style or express your personality through decor, these prints are made for you.

Simply print, frame, and decorate — it's that easy.

Thank you for being part of our Pinterest community. We hope this set helps you create a home that feels as good as it looks.

About the Creator

Decora was brought to life by a curious mind with a deep love for aesthetics, and clever design. With no formal background—just pure passion—Decora’s creator set out to turn everyday corners into art.
From Pinterest scrolls to late-night sketching, every piece of content is crafted with intention and a spark of creativity. Whether you're decorating a tiny room or dreaming up your future home, Decora is here to inspire your vision.
